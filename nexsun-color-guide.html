Do's & Don'ts

✦ Do

  • Use amber as the dominant accent color
  • Keep backgrounds warm cream or navy only
  • Use sky blue exclusively for ".ai" and secondary elements
  • Keep text either Navy or Text Muted
  • Add amber underline accents under "nexsun"
  • Use Outfit (900) for the brand name

✗ Don't

  • Use green for "nexsun.ai" link text (old version)
  • Use a dark starfield background
  • Mix warm + cool backgrounds
  • Use sky blue for large fills or backgrounds
  • Use grey tones keep neutrals warm
  • Use bold red or purple anywhere
