How TwoSuns Works

From Signal to Decision

TwoSuns coordinates how organizations interpret signals, align stakeholders, and execute across complex environments. The platform connects data, intelligence, and workflows into a continuous coordination system. Each stage builds context, refines direction, and enables orchestrated action.

The system is not limited to a single workflow or sequence. Organizations can enter at any stage, iterate continuously, and apply the platform across multiple initiatives simultaneously.

A Continuous Coordination System

Sense → Analyze → Position → Align → Commit → Optimize

TwoSuns operates as a continuous and adaptive coordination system. It connects signals to structured decisions, workflows to stakeholders, and decisions to execution and accountability.

The system is non-linear. Organizations can enter at any stage and apply it across multiple initiatives simultaneously.

Stage 01

Sense: Continuous Signal Awareness

TwoSuns ingests and synthesizes vast volumes of structured and unstructured data across global markets.

This includes:

  • Market activity and pricing signals
  • News, policy, and regulatory developments
  • Operational and supply chain indicators
  • Macroeconomic and geopolitical inputs
The system identifies what is changing continuously and surfaces what matters.
Stage 02

Analyze: Structured Intelligence and Signal Extraction

Signals are contextualized using AI and established frameworks, including multi-lens analysis and PESTLE.

TwoSuns evaluates:

  • Trade opportunities and positioning shifts
  • Risk exposure and scenario impact
  • Operational constraints and disruptions
  • Policy and regulatory implications
This transforms raw information into decision-grade intelligence.
Stage 03

Position: Workflow Orchestration and Decision Structuring

Insights are converted into structured positions.

Within TwoSuns:

  • Positions are logged and tracked over time
  • Strategies are developed and refined within a unified canvas
  • Multiple scenarios and perspectives are combined
  • Decisions are prepared for advancement through defined workflows
This is where analysis becomes actionable.
Stage 04

Align: Structured Collaboration Across Stakeholders

Decisions are aligned across internal teams and external counterparties through secure, purpose-built environments.

TwoSuns Rooms enable:

  • Internal coordination and strategy development
  • Negotiation and commercial alignment
  • Controlled information sharing and syndication
  • Review and approval processes
Collaboration is embedded directly into the decision system.
Stage 05

Commit: Advancing Decisions Through Controlled Workflows

Positions and strategies move forward through structured execution pathways.

This includes:

  • Publishing positions within the system
  • Advancing commercial discussions
  • Linking to contract development and negotiation
  • Preparing for downstream execution in systems of record
TwoSuns is not intended to replace execution systems. It is built to ensure decisions are ready for execution and that decision logic is enduring.
Stage 06

Optimize: Continuous Improvement

Every decision, input, and outcome is captured, then turned into learning that sharpens the next cycle.

TwoSuns provides:

  • Outcome measurement against original intent and assumptions
  • Feedback loops that refine models, playbooks, and decision logic
  • A persistent, auditable record that compounds into institutional memory
  • Continuous improvement across decisions, teams, and time
Each cycle strengthens the next, so decisions become faster, sharper, and better grounded.

In Practice

See It in Practice

Three examples of how the lifecycle connects in real operating environments.

1

Trade Opportunity Identification and Positioning

A trading team is monitoring shifts in regional supply and pricing dynamics.

⌄
Sense
TwoSuns detects changes in logistics flows, pricing spreads, and policy signals across multiple regions.
Analyze
The platform evaluates the impact on supply-demand balance, pricing differentials, and counterparty behavior using multi-lens analysis.
Position
A structured trade position is developed within the Position module, incorporating scenarios, timing, and risk considerations.
Align
The strategy is reviewed internally and shared with relevant stakeholders through Rooms for validation and refinement.
Commit
The position is advanced, and commercial discussions are initiated with counterparties.
Record
All inputs, assumptions, and decisions are logged for traceability and future reference.
Outcome

Faster identification of opportunity and a orchestrated path from insight to execution.

2

Risk Exposure and Scenario Response

A portfolio manager needs to assess exposure to a rapidly evolving geopolitical and policy environment.

⌄
Sense
TwoSuns captures policy developments, regulatory signals, and market reactions continuously.
Analyze
The system evaluates scenario impacts across positions, counterparties, and regions.
Position
Risk mitigation strategies are structured, including hedging, reallocation, or contract adjustments.
Align
Risk, trading, and executive teams collaborate within Rooms to align on response.
Commit
Decisions are advanced through controlled workflows and prepared for execution in downstream systems.
Record
A full audit trail is maintained, including rationale and decision pathways.
Outcome

Improved risk visibility and orchestrated response under time-sensitive conditions.

3

Contract Structuring and Negotiation

A commercial team is developing and negotiating a complex supply agreement.

⌄
Sense
TwoSuns surfaces relevant market conditions, pricing benchmarks, and policy considerations.
Analyze
Contract terms are evaluated against current and forward-looking market scenarios.
Position
A structured commercial position is developed, linking strategy to contractual terms.
Align
Negotiation Rooms enable controlled engagement with counterparties, while internal stakeholders review and approve terms.
Commit
Contract offers are generated, refined, and advanced through the workflow.
Record
All negotiations, changes, and decisions are captured in the system of decision record.
Outcome

Stronger commercial positioning and improved alignment between strategy and contract execution.
