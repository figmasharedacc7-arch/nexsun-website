Nexsun.ai is active on the global conference circuit, connecting with founders, investors, operators, and enterprise buyers across North America, the Middle East, and beyond.

SaaS North
Attended
Ottawa, Canada

Market Validation, Early Client Wins, and Momentum for AEPG

We attended SaaS North to engage directly with one of Canada's most relevant communities of founders, operators, investors, and growth-focused leaders. The conference gave us the opportunity to build meaningful relationships, test market response, and have direct conversations with decision-makers who understand both the challenges and opportunities of scaling in today's environment.

One of the most important outcomes from the conference was that we found our first clients there. Securing our first clients through the conference was a strong signal that the market is responding not only to the vision behind AEPG, but also to the practical value we can deliver. The conference also created promising conversations around investment potential.

We plan to return in 2026 and build on that momentum further, with the goal of having our CEO, Aiman El-Ramly, speak at the conference as part of AEPG's continued thought leadership and market presence.

Kuwait Oil & Gas Show
Attended
Kuwait

Ground-Level Market Learning, Client Traction, and Regional Expansion

Our team attended the Kuwait Oil and Gas Show with a clear objective: to be on the ground, learn directly from the market, and engage with the leaders, operators, and decision-makers shaping the region's energy and industrial sectors. The conference gave us direct exposure to the realities facing companies across drilling, oil production, and industrial operations.

One of the most important outcomes from KOGS was that we secured clients through the conference. The traction that came out of KOGS contributed directly to our market expansion. Working with clients across drilling, oil-producing, and cement-related industries further validated Nexsun.ai as an energy- and commodities-agnostic platform capable of supporting complex decision environments across the broader industrial landscape.

KOGS also helped deepen our understanding of how the regional market wants to engage. The strongest signals pointed toward practical value, explainable intelligence, and solutions that can support better decisions without forcing disruption.

STEP Dubai
Exhibited
Dubai, UAE

Exhibiting in a Fast-Moving AI Ecosystem and Expanding Our GCC Reach

We attended STEP Dubai to exhibit, engage with the regional market, and experience firsthand the distinct pace and openness shaping AI adoption across the GCC. One of the strongest takeaways was the maturity and ambition of Dubai's AI ecosystem. The market showed a high level of openness to new ideas, emerging technologies, and enterprise applications of AI.

At the same time, the conference confirmed something equally important: we are up to speed. Being in that environment, having those conversations, and presenting what we are building showed us that our direction, thinking, and market relevance are aligned with where this ecosystem is already going.

STEP Dubai also helped further expand our reach in the GCC. By exhibiting and participating in the conference, we were able to strengthen visibility, build new relationships, and continue extending our footprint in a region that remains strategically important for long-term growth.
