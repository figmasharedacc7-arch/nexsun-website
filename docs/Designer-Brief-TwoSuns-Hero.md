## 1. The company in 60 seconds

TwoSuns (formerly Nexsun.ai) is an AI-enhanced enterprise platform that operates as a **coordination infrastructure layer** across complex organizations. Where most enterprise software stores data or generates outputs, TwoSuns coordinates execution across systems, workflows, teams, and operational environments, with persistent context and AI-assisted alignment.

The platform has three pillars:
- **Core** — the continuity layer (persistent organizational memory, governance, the TwoSuns Assistant)
- **Horizon** — market and revenue execution (GTM, RevOps, contract workflows)
- **Forge** — operational execution (supply chain, procurement, scenario analysis)

The name "TwoSuns" references the cinematic Tatooine moment from Star Wars: possibility, ambition, exploration, persistent visibility. **Subtle homage, not literal sci-fi.**

## 2. Audience

Enterprise decision-makers in complex industries:
- C-suite (CEO, COO, CFO, CRO, CDO)
- Strategy and transformation leaders
- Heads of operations, procurement, GTM
- Risk, governance, compliance owners

They are sophisticated, time-poor, and skeptical of AI hype. They respond to weight, restraint, and competence. They are NOT looking for playful, startup, or consumer-style design.

## 3. Brand voice constraints

- **Enterprise-grade. Operational. Cinematic. Restrained. Quietly ambitious.**
- **Avoid:** consumer AI aesthetics, startup hype visuals, aggressive futurism, overt sci-fi styling, flashy interaction design
- **No em dashes anywhere.** Use commas. (CEO rule, enforced site-wide)
- **Tone:** Bloomberg confidence, not Slack-bot friendliness

Established positioning: "Coordination Infrastructure for Complex Enterprises."
Tagline: "Coordinate. Execute. Continue."
