The Interface

TwoSuns Assistant (TwoSuns Assistant)

TwoSuns Assistant is the primary interface to TwoSuns. It provides a role-aware, context-driven interaction layer that enables users to access intelligence, explore scenarios, and refine decisions continuously.

Users engage through text or voice. Interaction is conversational, iterative, and challengeable. Each response is grounded in identifiable sources, structured reasoning, and traceable logic.

Architecture

The Intelligence Engine

TwoSuns is powered by a data-forward AI architecture designed for real-world decision environments:

Nexus

The ingestion and normalization layer. Acquires structured and unstructured data from external and internal sources, converting signals into normalized "facts" for downstream processing.

Radar

The analytical intelligence layer. Responsible for signal detection, pattern recognition, and AI-assisted interpretation of market-relevant information across structured and unstructured sources.

Ledger

The TwoSuns Core. Preserves final analytical outputs, evidence chains, and decision artifacts in an immutable, auditable structure, WORM-compliant.
