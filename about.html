About Nexsun.ai

A Founder-Led Evolution of Decision Intelligence

A founder-led evolution toward structured decision systems. Nexsun.ai is built on more than three decades of experience operating in environments where data, timing, and decision quality directly impact outcomes.

Founder Track Record

Founded by Aiman El-Ramly, Nexsun.ai builds on a track record of creating and scaling category-defining platforms.

Zema Global Data Corporation
Global standard for market data integration and analytics across energy and commodities.
ZE Power Engineering
Full-service design engineering company and a BC anchor firm supporting critical utility infrastructure.
The Structural Gap

Markets have become more complex. Data has expanded rapidly, particularly unstructured data across policy, logistics, and market activity.

Existing systems were not designed to interpret or act on this in real time.

Nexsun.ai was built to define the next layer.

A system where market signals, strategy, and execution operate together.

Founder Perspective

"Over time, one pattern becomes clear. Organizations invest in data, systems, and talent. Each improves independently. Decision-making continues to rely on fragmented views and coordination across functions."

The constraint is structural. The challenge is not access to information. The challenge is how decisions are formed, aligned, executed, and retained. Nexsun.ai was built to address that gap directly.

— Aiman El-Ramly Founder & CEO
Product Perspective

"Most systems are designed to store data or generate outputs. Nexsun.ai is designed to structure how decisions are formed."

This requires an architecture that continuously ingests data, applies context, supports iteration, and connects intelligence directly to workflows and execution. The objective is consistency, clarity, and traceability across every decision.

— Ryan Arian Chief Digital Product Officer

Trust & Governance

Enterprise-Grade Security & Accountability

Enterprise buyers require more than intelligence, they require defensibility. Nexsun.ai is built governance-first, with auditability at every layer.

🔍

Glassbox AI™

Every AI-assisted insight has logged sources, reasoning paths, and assumptions. Nothing is opaque. Everything is reviewable and defensible under audit.

📒

System of Decision Record

Immutable SoDR preserves the complete decision chain from signal discovery to final outcome with WORM (Write Once, Read Many) storage compliance.

🏢

Tenant Isolation

Schema-per-tenant isolation in PostgreSQL provides structural separation of client data with full operational efficiency within shared infrastructure.

🔐

Encryption & Identity

TLS/mTLS for all communications, AWS KMS for data at rest, OIDC authentication, JWT authorization, and enterprise IdP integration.

📋

Audit Logging

Event-level audit trails, infrastructure-level access governance, and immutable evidence storage meeting enterprise compliance expectations.

👤

Human-in-the-Loop

AI-assisted extraction is always reviewable and verified before becoming operationally binding. Intelligence augments human judgment, it does not replace it.
