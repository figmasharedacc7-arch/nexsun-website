What this draft is testing

The whole scene is built in CSS and SVG so it renders offline with no assets. The motion you see (breathing suns, drifting light, film grain, slow god rays) is the cinematic feel we would carry into real video.

To make it genuinely realistic, three slots take real assets: a hero background clip (an industrial horizon at golden hour, the two suns graded in over it), the four vertical clips in the band above, and a set of stills for deeper pages. The silhouette here is a stand-in for that horizon footage.

Palette is the official set (orange #FF6600, gold #CC9900, beige #FFF5DD). Copy uses the confirmed tagline and the agreed industrial framing, so nothing here is blocked on the positioning sign-off. Swap to "complex industries" or "complex organizations" in one line when that lands.
