What changed from v1

The faked CSS sky, the two glowing div suns, and the SVG silhouette are gone. The hero now plays real generated footage: an industrial horizon at golden hour with the two suns shimmering through drifting haze, a slow push-in, rising smoke, and god rays. It autoplays muted, loops, and falls back to a poster still on slow connections.

The grain, vignette, and reading scrim from v1 are kept on top of the video, so the headline holds its contrast over the bright sky. Motion respects prefers-reduced-motion. Palette is the official set (orange #FF6600, gold #CC9900, beige #FFF5DD); copy is unchanged from v1.
