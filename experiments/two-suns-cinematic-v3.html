What changed from v2

The hero is no longer a flat loop behind text. It now plays an entrance: the footage blooms up from black, a calibration line sweeps across, an orbital chart draws itself around the two suns with mono coordinate readouts, then the headline wipes up line by line and the controls settle in. After the sequence it breathes, the orbit slowly rotates, embers drift, and every layer parallaxes to the cursor for depth.

The instrument layer is the brand's Heliocentric Cartography made literal: registration frame, corner ticks, a great orbit, the two bodies labelled α and β as market and operations. Hairlines are helio-gold only, kept faint so the headline always wins. Motion fully respects prefers-reduced-motion.

Open items: tune the orbit so it tracks the suns at every viewport, a perfectly seamless video loop, and the four vertical clips. Say the word.
