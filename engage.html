Start a Pilot

Validate Nexsun.ai in your operating environment

Nexsun.ai pilots are designed to demonstrate value across real workflows, including trading, operations, and strategic decision-making.

Pilot Structure
  • 3-6 month engagement
  • Defined use case (trade, risk, operations, or strategy)
  • Integration with relevant data and systems
  • Direct collaboration with Nexsun.ai team
Outcomes
  • Measurable impact on decision speed and quality
  • Alignment across stakeholders
  • Clear path to production deployment
Commercial Model Typically structured as a paid engagement in the range of $25,000 to $100,000+, depending on scope and complexity.
Example Pilot Applications
  • LNG portfolio optimization across basins and pricing benchmarks (improved margin capture and faster position alignment)
  • Industrial plant margin optimization (reduced input cost variability and improved production decisions)
  • Portfolio risk assessment under shifting policy conditions (earlier risk identification and coordinated response)
  • Contract structuring aligned to forward market scenarios (stronger commercial positioning and negotiation outcomes)

Pilot engagements are initiated on a limited basis each quarter to ensure depth of collaboration and delivery quality.
