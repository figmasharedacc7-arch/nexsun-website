Why Nexsun.ai

Why people join our team.

We're a small team with deep domain expertise, a genuine product, and a market that needs us. Here's what that means for you.

🎯

Real Domain Depth

Our founders spent 30+ years building Zema Global, one of the world's leading energy data platforms. You'll learn from people who've actually built and scaled in this industry.

🚀

Early-Stage Ownership

You won't be a cog in a machine. Every person on the team shapes the product, the culture, and the direction. Your work will be visible and your impact measurable.

🌍

Market That Matters

Energy markets move the global economy. The decisions we help people make have real consequences for businesses, for communities, for the energy transition.

🧠

Cutting-Edge AI

We're building explainable, enterprise-grade AI, not hype. Glassbox AI™, SoDR immutable records, and real-time multi-dimensional signal processing.

⚖️

Flexible & Remote-First

Headquartered in Richmond, BC, but we hire for talent, not geography. We trust you to do great work wherever you do it best.

📈

Equity & Growth

As an early team member, you'll share in the upside. We're building for the long term, and the people who help build it should benefit from it.
