Governance & Security

Trust, Governance, and Decision Accountability

Nexsun.ai is designed for environments where decisions must be explainable, traceable, and aligned with enterprise governance standards.

Core Trust Pillars

Built for Enterprise Accountability

Nexsun.ai is built to ensure every decision is explainable, traceable, and governed across its full lifecycle.

🔍

Glassbox AI Explainability

Full transparency into data, logic, and assumptions behind every output, enabling explainable and defensible decisions.

📒

System of Decision Record (SoDR)

A persistent system capturing every input, assumption, and decision, creating a complete and auditable record of how outcomes are formed.

🏢

Tenant Isolation

Secure, tenant-specific environments ensuring complete data separation and controlled access.

🔐

Encryption & Identity

Enterprise-grade encryption and identity management supporting secure access, authentication, and integration.

📋

Audit Logging

Full visibility into system activity, decision workflows, and data access, supporting governance and compliance.

👤

Human-in-the-Loop

Critical decisions remain governed by human oversight, with AI augmenting judgment within structured workflows.

Architecture Security

Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure

Nexsun.ai is deployed on a cloud-native architecture designed for scalability, resilience, and enterprise-grade performance.

Cloud Infrastructure
Built on AWS with enterprise-grade scalability, resilience, and global availability.
Data Architecture
Designed to process large volumes of structured and unstructured data in real time.
AI Infrastructure
Supports advanced models and continuous analysis across complex datasets.
Security Framework
Enterprise-grade access controls, encryption, and identity management across all system layers.
🔗

Nexus

The ingestion and normalization layer. Acquires structured and unstructured data from external and internal sources, converting signals into normalized "facts" for downstream processing.

📡

Radar

The analytical intelligence layer. Responsible for signal detection, pattern recognition, and AI-assisted interpretation of market-relevant information across structured and unstructured sources.

📒

Ledger

The System of Decision Record (SoDR). Preserves final analytical outputs, evidence chains, and decision artifacts in an immutable, auditable structure, WORM-compliant.
