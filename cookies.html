Cookie Policy

A clear explanation of the cookies Nexsun.ai uses, why we use them, and the controls available to you. We use no advertising cookies only what is necessary to deliver and improve your experience.

Cookie Policy Last updated: April 2026 No Advertising Cookies

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, to improve efficiency, and to provide information to the site operators. Cookies can be "session" cookies, which expire when you close your browser, or "persistent" cookies, which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

Similar technologies such as web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage operate in comparable ways and are covered by this Policy. Together with cookies, these are referred to as "tracking technologies."

2. Cookie Types We Use

Nexsun.ai uses three categories of cookies. We do not use advertising or targeting cookies of any kind.

🔒
Essential

Essential Cookies

Strictly necessary for the platform to function. These enable authentication, session security, and access control. You cannot opt out of these cookies while using the platform.

📊
Analytics

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website and platform which pages are visited, how long sessions last, and where errors occur. Data is aggregated and anonymised.

⚙️
Preferences

Preference Cookies

Remember your choices and settings such as language preference, dashboard theme, and UI configuration, so you don't have to reconfigure them on each visit.

4. No Advertising Cookies

Nexsun.ai does not use advertising, retargeting, or cross-site tracking cookies. We do not partner with advertising networks, social media platforms (for tracking purposes), or data brokers. Your activity on Nexsun.ai is never used to target you with advertisements on other websites or platforms.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use a limited set of third-party analytics tools to help us understand how the platform is used. These providers may set their own cookies on your device. All third-party analytics providers engaged by Nexsun.ai are contractually bound to process data solely for analytics purposes and are prohibited from using data for advertising or sharing it with other third parties.

  • Analytics providers: Anonymised, aggregated usage data only no personally identifiable information is shared with analytics tools
  • No social media trackers: We do not embed social media tracking pixels from Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, or similar platforms
  • No advertising networks: We do not use Google Ads, Meta Ads, programmatic advertising, or any equivalent ad-tech cookies

6. How to Manage and Disable Cookies

You have several options for controlling cookies:

6.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies through their settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging into the Nexsun.ai platform. Below are links to cookie management guides for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

6.2 Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit Nexsun.ai, a cookie consent banner is displayed. You may accept all cookies or decline non-essential cookies (analytics and preferences). Your selection is stored in your browser's local storage and honoured on future visits. You may change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or contacting us at privacy@nexsun.ai.

6.3 Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals from browsers. When a DNT signal is detected, Nexsun.ai will not load analytics cookies beyond those that are strictly essential for platform operation.

7. Cookies and Personal Data

Where cookies collect or process personal data, such processing is governed by our Privacy Policy. Analytics cookies collect data in anonymised or pseudonymised form and do not directly identify you. Preference and essential cookies may be associated with your account where you are logged in to the platform.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our platform. When material changes are made, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We recommend reviewing this Policy periodically.
