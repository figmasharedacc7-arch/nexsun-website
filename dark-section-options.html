TwoSuns Design Review

Dark section alternatives

Twelve warm treatments for the site's dark bands, all showing the same section and copy so only the background changes. The brief: keep the contrast, lose the dark. Option 1 is what the site uses today. Options 2 to 12 are the first round; 13 to 16 pull from the secondary palette (gold, sun-pale, soft yellow, and the dark Sky accent). Jump between them below.
