### WHY NEXSUN.AI (why-nexsun.html) - NEW

**Hero Heading:** "Defining the Decision Intelligence Layer"

**Hero Subheading:** "Nexsun.ai is built for a structural shift in how organizations operate in complex markets. Data has scaled. Systems have multiplied. Decision-making remains fragmented. Nexsun.ai addresses this gap by establishing a unified layer where market signals, strategy, and execution converge."

**Section: A Different Category** - Three existing platform categories:
1. Systems of Record (ETRM, CTRM, ERP): "Manage data and transactions. Essential for operational accuracy and compliance record-keeping."
2. Data and Analytics Platforms: "Aggregate and interpret data. Provide analytical support for understanding historical and predictive trends."
3. Workflow and Collaboration Tools: "Manage processes and communication. Enable teams to coordinate tasks and share information."

Key statement: "These systems are necessary. They are not sufficient. They manage data and transactions. They do not manage decisions. Nexsun.ai defines the decision intelligence layer."
